Defenses

The town hall has a dedicated Militia section. Unfortunately not too many people in the town are capable of fighting, so there are only 14 Soldiers, and Varstrum the cleric. On occasion, the R.I.C. Officer Apostolies and his battalion of 6 guards patrol the town and can be around if needed, but Apostolies can be busy patrolling other towns, checking in at Fort Moonlight, or investigating Daggershine so he is not readily available.

Notable Areas

Hollowgreen inn

Though once a thriving tavern, an unfortunate incident involving a poker game and a fight lead to the tavern being burnt down.

Drunken Fools Tavern

This scummy craphole of a bar served as the party’s temporary home while in Ambermeadow. The barkeep of the Drunken fool tavern is a sleazebag named Lorsan Yeshana. He offers what he calls “The house special” which is a type of drink he brews that he claims can make you drunk basically instantly and with no hangover in the morning. It is a horrifying concoction of the worst drugs imaginable, and is guaranteed to kill you if you have more than 3.
